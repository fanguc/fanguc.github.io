Research

Infinitely many supersingular primes for some Mumford's abelian fourfolds

We generalize Elkies’ theorem on infinitude of supersingular primes for elliptic curves to some abelian fourfolds in Mumford’s families, and more generally, to certain families of Kuga-Satake abelian varieties.
